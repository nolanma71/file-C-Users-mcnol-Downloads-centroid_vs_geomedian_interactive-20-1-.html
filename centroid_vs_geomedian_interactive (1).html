Centers Explained

Centroid: Average of coordinates. Quick to compute but can be skewed by outliers.

Geometric Median: Minimizes total distance to all points. More robust to outliers.

Distance Concepts

Haversine Distance: Straight-line distance over Earth's surface.

Driving Distance: Real-world travel distance along roads (requires API).

This demo uses Haversine distance only.
